Life Insurance Calculator

Life insurance companies quote premiums based on age, sex, health, and tobacco use — I built a quiz that calculates that quoted age without being boring or tedious. Custom illustrations and a casual, familiar tone keep each simple screen quick to get through, with progress dots showing how much of the quiz is left so users always know how much longer it'll take.

Landing Pages

While working as the Senior Designer at Brigham and Women's Hospital, I designed several landing pages for fundraising events and groups. The micro-sites usually consisted of a landing page, an info page and a donation form. The pages were meant to inspire members of the community to get involved and donate to causes championed by the hospital.
