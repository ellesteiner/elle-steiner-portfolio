Company Home, About + Careers Page Redesign

Life Insurance Calculator

The Task: Create a quiz that calculates the age that life insurance companies use to quote insurance premiums, based on personal information, such as sex, age, health and tobacco use.

The Challenge: Make life insurance NOT boring and tedious.

The Solution: In order to make this life insurance calculator more fun, I created custom illustrations and used a familiar, casual tone with the content and micro-text. Each screen is simple, straightforward, and easy to interact with. The progress dots on the bottom let the user know how many screens are left, so they are not wondering how much longer the quiz will take. The result is a quick and easy quiz that lets people know how life insurance companies will view them when providing life insurance quotes.
